Topic: Bare Text Traps

	Symbol that are part of the sentence structure should not become part of bare links.

	Text in parentheses (http://www.naturaldocs.org) and (email@address.com).

	Text in quotes "http://www.naturaldocs.org" and "email@address.com".

	Ending with period http://www.naturaldocs.org.  And email@address.com.

	Ending with comma http://www.naturaldocs.org, and email@address.com, and stuff.
	
	Ending with exclamation point http://www.naturaldocs.org!  And email@address.com!

	Ending with question mark http://www.naturaldocs.org?  And email@address.com?

	Ending with a combo ("http://www.naturaldocs.org.")  And (email@address.com.)
